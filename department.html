About Government Polytechnic Nainital

Government Polytechnic Nainital, established in 1957, recently celebrated its 67th anniversary of imparting high-quality education to the students of Uttarakhand.
G.P Nainital is affiliated with the Uttarakhand Board of Technical Education, Roorkee.
