About G.P. Nainital

Government Polytechnic Nainital was established in 1957, making it one of the oldest technical institutes in Uttarakhand. G.P. Nainital is widely recognized as one of the best institutions for technical education in the state. The college offers a variety of diploma programs catering to multiple engineering and technical fields. Its curriculum is thoughtfully designed to bridge the gap between theoretical academics and hands-on industry skills. The institute emphasizes both classroom learning and practical training to prepare students for real-world challenges. G.P. Nainital boasts a strong faculty team comprising experienced educators and industry professionals. Industry experts frequently collaborate with the college to provide students with up-to-date knowledge and training. The college maintains a vast and active alumni network spread across top industries in India and abroad.
Government Polytechnic Nainital (known as Nainital Polytechnic) is a technical education institute located in Nainital, Uttarakhand. Established in 1975, the institute is affiliated with the Uttarakhand Technical Education Board, Roorkee and All India Council For Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi.

Courses offered by the institute include Civil Engineering, PGDCA, Electrical Engineering, Electronics Engineering, Information Technology, Mechanical Engineering, Modern Office Management & Secretarial Practice, and Pharmacy. Before the affiliation with the Uttarakhand Technical Education Board, the institute was affiliated with UP Technical Board, Lucknow.

GENESIS
