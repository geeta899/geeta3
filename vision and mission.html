VISION & MISSION

VISION

To groom students into technically sound individuals with high ethical standards committed towards the vision of self-reliance.

MISSION

Imparting quality, employment oriented modern technical education.
Inculcating the spirit of entrepreneurial professionalism.
Remaining relevant to welfare of the nation and mankind.

Government Polytechnic Nainital

Government Polytechnic Nainital, established in 1957, recently celebrated its 67th anniversary of imparting high quality education to the students of Uttarakhand. G.P Nainital is associated with Uttarakhand Board of Technical Education, Roorkee.
